Multi-orientation Pedestrian Detection

This dataset is designed for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and focuses on detecting pedestrians in eight walking orientations: Front, Back, Left, Right, Left-Front, Left-Back, Right-Front, Right-Back.
