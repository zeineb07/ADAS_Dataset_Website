Multi-orientation Pedestrian Detection

This dataset is designed for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). The dataset was collected from different Moroccan cities and focuses on detecting pedestrians in eight walking orientations: Front, Back, Left, Right, Left-Front, Left-Back, Right-Front, Right-Back, using an industrial camera with a resolution of 2.3 megapixels and 60 frames per second, on board a moving vehicle.
